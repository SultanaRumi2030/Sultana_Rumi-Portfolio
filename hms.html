Hospital Management System

About This Project

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a web-based solution designed to streamline hospital operations. It helps manage patient records, appointments, staff, billing, inventory, and reporting. The HMS improves healthcare services by making data accessible, secure, and easy to manage across departments.

Electronic Health Records

Maintain complete digital records of patients for easy access and better decision-making.

Staff & Patient Management

Organize staff details, manage schedules, and handle patient information seamlessly.

Billing & Inventory

Automates billing processes and tracks medical inventory to reduce errors and costs.
