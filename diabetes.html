Diabetes Prediction Using Machine Learning

About This Project

This project uses **Machine Learning models** to predict the likelihood of a person developing diabetes based on medical and lifestyle data. By analyzing factors such as age, BMI, blood pressure, and glucose levels, the model provides an early warning system that helps doctors and patients make better health decisions.

Machine Learning Models

Trained with logistic regression, decision trees, and random forest classifiers for accurate predictions.

Data Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of patient datasets to identify key risk factors of diabetes.

Healthcare Impact

Assists in early diagnosis to prevent severe complications and improve patient lifestyle.
